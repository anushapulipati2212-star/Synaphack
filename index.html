About Synaphack

Synaphack is a community-driven platform where security researchers, students, and engineers come together to learn, compete, and build. We host monthly events, mentorship cohorts, and open-source projects focused on improving tooling and defensive practices.

Mission

Democratize practical security education and foster collaborations that make software safer.

Values

Respect, curiosity, responsible disclosure, and open-source first.
